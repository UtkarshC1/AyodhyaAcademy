Our Mission

A Foundation for Success

At Ayodhay Academy, we believe in a holistic approach to education. Our mission is to foster curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking in every student, preparing them for a bright future.

Academic Excellence

We are committed to providing a rigorous academic curriculum that challenges students to reach their full potential.

Community & Spirit

Our school is a vibrant community where students, teachers, and parents work together to create a supportive environment.

Personal Growth

Beyond academics, we focus on developing character, leadership skills, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Curriculum

CBSE-Affiliated Education

As a CBSE-affiliated school, we follow a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to Class 12, fostering intellectual, emotional, and physical growth.

Primary (Nursery - 5)

A nurturing environment that focuses on foundational skills, play-based learning, and curiosity.

Middle (6 - 8)

Our curriculum deepens critical thinking and subject knowledge, preparing students for senior grades.

Senior (9 - 12)

We offer diverse streams and personalized guidance to help students excel in their board examinations and future careers.
